About us

We are a small citrus packing house in Orosi, California, in business since 1973.

Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality, great tasting, and healthy citrus products.

We offer custom packing services and also have access to more than 1,500 acres of land where we grow lemons, mandarins, oranges, grapefruits - and their respective varietals.

Our greatest commitment is to our consumers' health. We promote food safety and are certified by Primus Labs.
